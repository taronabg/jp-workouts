ABOUT JACOB PERRY

Former professional athlete turned elite fitness trainer, dedicated to transforming lives through personalized training programs and unwavering commitment to excellence.

My Journey

My name is Jacob Perry, raised in the projects in a low-income neighborhood—my circumstances never defined me. From a young age, I was determined to rise above, both in the classroom and on the court. I was honored to be invited to a Duke University talent search program after scoring among the top nationally on the SAT. But my true passion was always basketball.

I went 35–1 during my freshman year playing college ball in Michigan, winning a national championship. I later transferred to Shelton State for my sophomore season, finishing 25–8, and continued my journey at Alabama A&M as a junior. I entered the NBA Draft after that year, and although I went undrafted and didn’t play my senior season, I’ve never stopped grinding.

I'm known for my motto of playing with "heart over height"—leaving everything on the floor, every game. Whether it’s hitting clutch game-winners, locking up on defense, or finding the open teammate, I pride myself on doing whatever it takes to win.

Off the court, I’m a proud father of four: Jacoby Perry (13), JaBria Perry (9), Grace Perry (3), and Roman Perry (1). They are my daily motivation. I also carry the legacy of my father, Willie J. Perry, a professional boxer who once fought on ESPN and passed away on May 23, 2025. His fight lives on in me.

This is more than a game. It’s purpose, passion, and family.
